CGPA Calculator For Everyone

The Cumulative Grade Point Average (CGPA) is a vital academic metric used by universities worldwide to measure a student's overall academic performance. This standardized grading system provides a comprehensive view of your academic achievements throughout your degree program.

Our specialized CGPA calculator follows the standard 4.0 grading scale, allowing you to accurately calculate your CGPA based on your course credit hours and grades. Whether you're studying at a university or college, this tool implements the standard grading scale used by most institutions.

Why CGPA Matters

Your CGPA is crucial for scholarship applications, postgraduate admissions, and job opportunities. Many institutions require a minimum CGPA for admission to Masters and PhD programs. Employers also frequently use CGPA as a screening criterion for fresh graduates.

Understanding your CGPA helps you track your academic progress, set realistic goals, and maintain the grades needed for your educational and career aspirations. Use this calculator each semester to monitor your performance and make informed decisions about your academic journey.

Understanding CGPA in Pakistani Universities

In Pakistan's higher education system, CGPA serves as the primary indicator of academic performance. Most universities follow a standardized 4.0 scale with specific grading policies:

  • A (4.0): Excellent (85-100%)
  • A- (3.7): Very Good (80-84%)
  • B+ (3.5): Good (75-79%)
  • B (3.3): Above Average (70-74%)
  • B- (3.0): Average (65-69%)
  • C+ (2.7): Below Average (60-64%)
  • C (2.3): Marginal (55-59%)
  • C- (2.0): Pass (50-54%)
  • D (1.3): Conditional Pass (45-49%)
  • F (0.0): Fail (Below 45%)

This calculator accurately implements the grading system used by HEC-recognized universities across Pakistan, including University of the Punjab, Karachi University, and Quaid-i-Azam University.
